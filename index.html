With the Amount of People Experiencing Homelessness

As of 2021, there are over 552,830 people who are unhoused in this country (This only includes those who are accounted for).

When You Consider the Raw Numbers,

This means that 17 people per 10,000 currently experience homelessness everday.

Among this group, There Lies Another:

The chronically unhoused. This group of people account for 19% of the Unhoused Population.

How Does Nashville Compare?

As of January 2022

The City of Nashville has accounted for 1900 individuals who are experiencing homelessness. Of these, 600 are chronically unhoused.

What Does it Mean to be Chronically Unhoused?

That means any person who has been constantly experiencing homeless for at least a year.

What Does This Mean for Nashville:

Our chronically homeless account for 26%! Almost 10% HIGHER than the national average!

What Has Been the Constant Approach?

Buses. Greyhound buses to be exact. While there has been a push to address the issue with one of of our most at risk populations, the prevailing solution that most major cities participate in involves this: Gathering the unhoused and bussing them to different cities. This has continued for the last 40+ years. Back in 2017, The Guardian released a revealing article that layed out what happen after they did an 18-month investigation.

This is Where The Unity Project Comes in!

What Is The Unity Project?

  • The Unity Project is a service that would assists loved one in their search to reconnect with those who were unhoused.
  • How it works: Access our database of bus records, limited access medical records, and other public access files.
  • Once you find a match, our application will narrow your search window to a specific region of the country.
  • With this in mind, you can get a strong start in your search in locating your unhoused loved ones!
  • *This is project that is still in development*
